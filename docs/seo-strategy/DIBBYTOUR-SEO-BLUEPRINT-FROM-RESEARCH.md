# PART 1: THE EXACT KEYWORD FORMULAS THAT WORK

## RESEARCH FINDINGS

### What Wise Did (60M monthly visits):
- Formula: `[Currency 1] to [Currency 2]` 
- Example: "USD to EUR", "GBP to USD"
- Why it worked: REAL-TIME DATA on every page (exchange rates)
- Pages created: 10,000+ currency pair pages

### What Zapier Did (8M monthly visits):
- Formula: `[App 1] + [App 2] integration`
- Example: "Slack Gmail integration", "Google Sheets Trello"
- Why it worked: 3-tier structure (App → App+App → Specific Workflow)
- Pages created: 50,000+ programmatic pages

### What Nomad List Did (43K monthly visits):
- Formula: `Cost of living in [City]`, `Best cities for [Audience]`
- Why it worked: UNIQUE DATA (internet speed, safety scores, cost breakdown)
- Pages created: 24,000+ city pages

### What TripAdvisor Did (226M monthly visits):
- Formula: `Best [Service] in [City]`
- Example: "Best restaurants in Chicago", "Things to do in Paris"
- Why it worked: USER-GENERATED reviews + comprehensive coverage
- Pages created: Millions of location-based pages
